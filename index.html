Teaching focusing on practical considered game design, taking game design seriously as a formal design practice, and game analysis.

My work centres on helping students understand how games are made, funded, and distributed — and the ethical implications of those systems.

I teach realistic design practice like it's making a product to be used by an audience, and focus on truly understanding the function and use of interactive game systems.
